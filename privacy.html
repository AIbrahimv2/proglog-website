Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

ProgLog! ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the ProgLog! mobile application (the "App") handles your information. In short: the App does not collect or transmit your workout data. Everything you log stays on your device.

2. Information the App Handles

2.1 Workout and fitness data (stored locally on your device)

When you use the App, you create data such as:

  • Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout dates you log.
  • Programs, training sessions, equipment, and locations you create.
  • Bodyweight entries you record.
  • App settings and preferences.

All of this is stored locally on your device in an on-device database. It is not uploaded to any server operated by us. We do not see it, and we have no ability to access it remotely. If you use iCloud backup (iOS) or Google backup (Android), this data may be included in your device backup according to Apple's or Google's respective privacy practices.

The App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any account identifiers. No account is required to use the App.

2.2 Support inquiries

If you contact us through the support form on our website, we receive the name, email address, and message you provide so we can respond to you. Support submissions are handled by our form provider (Formspree) and delivered to our email. They are not used for marketing or shared with anyone else.

2.3 What the App does NOT collect

The App does not include:

  • Analytics, tracking, or telemetry SDKs.
  • Crash reporting services.
  • Advertising identifiers or ad networks.
  • A custom backend or cloud sync service operated by us.

3. Apple Health (HealthKit), Optional

On iOS, the App can optionally integrate with Apple Health if you grant permission. If you enable this, the App may:

  • Read active energy burned and body mass samples from Apple Health.
  • Write completed workout sessions and manual bodyweight entries back to Apple Health.

HealthKit syncs happen on-device only, when triggered by actions you take (finishing a session, logging bodyweight, or using the "Sync History" button). Health data is never transmitted to us or to any third party. You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health. HealthKit is not available on Android.

4. In-App Purchases

If the App offers in-app purchases or premium features, they are processed through RevenueCat, a third-party payment and subscription management service. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat receives limited information needed to validate and manage your purchase (such as a per-install anonymous user ID, your App Store or Play Store purchase receipt, and your entitlement status). RevenueCat does not receive your workout data, your name, or your email address from the App. You can review RevenueCat's privacy practices at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Payments themselves are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) according to their respective terms and privacy policies. We do not see or store your payment details.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent any information to third parties. The only parties that receive any information at all are:

  • Apple or Google (for in-app purchases and, if enabled, iCloud/Google backups of your device).
  • Apple Health (if you grant HealthKit permission, on your device).
  • RevenueCat (for purchase validation, as described above).
  • Formspree (only if you contact us through the support form).

6. Your Rights and Controls

  • Delete your data. You can clear all workout data directly in the App at any time. Because your data lives on your device, deleting it there removes it entirely.
  • Revoke HealthKit access. You can turn off HealthKit permissions for ProgLog! at any time in the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health.
  • Uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes its local database and all associated data from your device.
  • Contact us with any questions about your data.

7. Data Security

Because workout data stays on your device, its security is primarily governed by your device's own protections (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, encryption, and OS-level sandboxing). For anything transmitted to us (such as a support request), we rely on HTTPS in transit. No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the App and on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@proglog.dev
  • Support page: proglog.dev/support
