Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Introduction

ProgLog ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ProgLog mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Workout Data: Exercises, sets, reps, weights, and workout dates that you log in the App.
  • Profile Information: Any optional profile details you choose to enter, such as your name or body weight.
  • Support Inquiries: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us through the support form.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to help us improve the App (e.g., which features are most used). These analytics do not identify you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (workout logging and progress tracking).
  • Respond to your support requests.
  • Improve the App's features and user experience.
  • Send important notices about changes to the App or our policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your workout data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your workout data on external servers. If you use iCloud backup, your data may be included in your device backup according to Apple's privacy practices.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information transmitted to us (e.g., support requests). However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you for analytics and improvement purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit: If you grant permission, ProgLog may read from or write to Apple Health. This data is handled according to Apple's HealthKit guidelines and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused analytics tools to collect anonymous usage data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete your data at any time by using the "Delete All Data" option in the App's settings.
  • Opt out of analytics collection in the App's settings.
  • Contact us with any questions about your data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy in the App and on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@proglog.dev
  • Support page: proglog.dev/support
